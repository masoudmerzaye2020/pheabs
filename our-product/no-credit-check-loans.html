How Can I Maximise the Chance of My Loan Being Approved?

To boost your chances of being accepted for a no credit loan, you should certainly have a stable income and be able to show proof of your earnings each month. This gives the lender confidence that you will have money in your account available each month for repayment.

In addition, if you can avoid getting too many loans or credit cards, this will make you seem less of a risk – otherwise it will raise some warning signs.

Be realistic in the amount that you wish to borrow. If you want to borrow $500, this should be realistic based on your salary. But if you are unemployed and have lots of debt, looking for $30,000 may not be sensible.

Plus, anything you can do to improve your credit score can only help you get the loan you need. Consider closing down any store cards, credit cards or accounts that you are not using. Think about paying off any debts that you can little-by-little. Maybe disassociate yourself that you share a bank account or mortgage with if they have bad credit. Finally, be sure to check your credit score regularly using free trials and tools, just to make sure that your score is always approving.
