What is a Payday Advance?

A payday advance refers to an advance on your monthly income and rather than waiting until your next payday from work, you can receive the money upfront. Also known as a cash advance, this type of loan is designed to help you pay for any pressing or immediate expenses, so you can receive the money in one lump sum upfront to pay off your expense and then repay the loan in full on your next payday from work. Some advances last just a few weeks, but some can last a few months, giving you the option to repay over monthly installments.
