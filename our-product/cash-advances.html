What is Cash Advance?

A cash advance is a type of short-term loan, offered by a range of different providers. However, this term is also used when referring to a service offered by credit card providers to their cardholders.

When discussing cash advance options here, Pheabs are referring to its status as a short-term loan, specifically as payday loans.

With these types of loans, the lender won’t necessarily base a loan request on the borrower’s credit score, but will also look at local state regulations as well as the borrower’s paycheck.
