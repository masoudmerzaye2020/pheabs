How Does it Work?

Pheabs offers a simple loan connection service – taking your loan requirements and matching them with the US-lender who is mostly likely to approve you and offer the best terms possible. Complete our online loan request form in less than 5 minutes and you will receive a provisional quote on the screen – and you can proceed with the loan offer if you want to – with no fees and no impact to your credit score. Further to some checks by the lender, successful applicants can receive money to their checking account within 24 hours, next business day or sometimes within 1 hour.
